How to post news for your department

Follow these steps to publish a news post on the DAG Industries intranet. Your post will appear on the homepage once it's live.

1

Go to your department site

Navigate to your department's SharePoint site. Make sure you're on the site you want the news to come from — the news will be tagged with that site's name.

Example site URL
awiadmin.sharepoint.com/sites/digitaldepartment
2

Click "+ New" at the top of the page

On the site homepage, look for the + New button in the top toolbar. Click it to open the dropdown menu.

3

Select "News post" — not "Page"

From the dropdown, select News post. Do not select "Page" — a regular page will not appear on the intranet homepage.

Why does this matter? News posts are flagged internally as promoted content. The intranet homepage only picks up items with this flag set.

4

Choose a layout and add your content

Pick a layout that suits your post. Then fill in:

Title Banner image Body text Author

Pro tip: Always add a banner image. Posts without an image show a generic placeholder on the homepage and get far less engagement.

5

Click "Post news" to publish

When you're done, click the Post news button in the top right — not just "Save as draft". Drafts will not appear on the homepage.

Important: "Publish" and "Post news" are different buttons. Only "Post news" makes your article visible on the intranet homepage. If you only see "Publish", look for a dropdown arrow next to it and select "Post news" from there.

6

Wait a few minutes for it to appear

After posting, your news will be indexed and should appear on the homepage within 15 – 30 minutes. If it hasn't appeared after an hour, contact the IT/Digital team.
